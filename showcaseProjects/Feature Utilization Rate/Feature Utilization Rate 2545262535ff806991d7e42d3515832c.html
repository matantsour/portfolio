Scenario:

TicketHero is a platform built for IT teams to manage internal support across the organization.
TicketHero helps IT provide faster, more efficient service whether employees need help with:

  • Resetting a password
  • Fixing a device
  • Getting access to a system

Today, employees can open IT support tickets in several ways, for example:

  • Emailing the helpdesk
  • Calling a technician
  • Using the self-service portal.

This assignment focuses on the self-service portal.

The self-service portal is designed to let employees report issues on their own.

To improve that experience, we recently introduced a new feature: an AI-powered chatbot embedded in the self-service portal.

Now, when employees visit the portal, they have two options:

2. Use the chatbot first, describing their issue in plain language

‚óè Understand the request
‚óè Suggest a solution, such as a help article or instructions

‚óè Or escalate the ticket to a technician if it can‚Äôt help

automatically, but it is marked as resolved immediately to reflect that no technician
